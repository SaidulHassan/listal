listal-dl

batch download photos from listal.com

Listal.com - List the stuff you love!

listal-dl is actually a script written in python 3 that makes use of external modules BeautifulSoup4 and LXML.

listal-dl can download all photos of any person from listal.com. It uses variable no. of threads for quick operations. See Threads.

After First step listal-dl creates a directory(folder) in current working dir and places the list of image links there. You can use your favourite downloader or utility like aria2 or wget to download photos. OR use listal-dl.
